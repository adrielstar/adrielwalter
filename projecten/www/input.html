Inputs

Variance in fly bit exposure:

The variation between individuals in exposure to fly bites (the infection is by flying transferred).

Pre-control CMFL:

The intensity of infection in the population prior to the start of mass treatment (CMFL).

Ivermectin assumption:

Assumptions about the effects of the drug ivermectin (two alternatives, the supplied data relate to one of those alternatives

Pastrounds:

Number of mass treatment rounds that has already taken place annually.

Coverage:

Population coverage (coverage) of mass treatment rounds have already taken place annually.
Population coverage (coverage) of future mass treatment rounds.

Percentage Line:

Chance of elimination in percentage. Adjustable from 90%, 95% or 99%.
